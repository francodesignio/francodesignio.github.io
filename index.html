I design.

My name is Franco Aguilera and I´m a technology oriented industrial designer.

I started as a Biomedical Engineer who focused on innovation projects, but decided to tackle innovation through a different perspective. I´ve always been equally inclined to STEM and artistic projects since I have a duality of mind. I am logical yet creative; I believe functionality and aesthetics are not mutually exclusive and equally important. My design process is highly methodicaI and attentive to detail, which helps the design translate efficiently with the complex engineering process.

I am currently working at SelfCare Medical Solutions as Director of Research & Development.
