Engineering Experience

I studied Biomedical Engineering for three years before I started as an Industrial Designer. I wanted to create things, to innovate. Tech projects are always conformed by a multidiciplinary team where designers and engineers work together, although they contribute in very different ways. I realized I loved the designer´s job, the creative begginings of projects. However, my engineering experience have been extremely useful in the design process of technological devices.

Here are some of my projects during my engineering years.

Arm Prosthesis

Designer / Developer

As a personal side project I designed and developed an electrical 3D printed low cost prosthesis, achieving the award Infomatrix Latinoamerica 2017.

Infant Arrhythmia Detector

Designer / Developer

As a personal side project I designed and developed an Infant Arrhythmia Detector prototype using an oxymeter. It received the local award Expo Ingeniería 2017.

Metallisation Equipment Co.

Mechanical Designer

I designed a cabin for recycling metallization waste. I also made manufacture blueprints and schematics.

Neonatal Intensive Care Unit Noise Detector

Mechanical Designer

In a team of engineers, we developed a noise detector for the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit. It was designed for the Hospital Civil de Guadalajara.
